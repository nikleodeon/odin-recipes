Egg and Tomato Stir-fry

Description

A homestyle classic made with scrambled eggs and tomatoes served with rice.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • Kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing rice wine or dry sherry
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 pound beefsteak tomatoes in season, or 1 14.5 ounce can of diced tomatoes in juice
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 scallions, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon minced ginger (from about 1/4 inch nub)
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Steps

  1. Beat eggs with salt, sesame oil, and rice wine.
  2. Stir together cornstarch and 2 tablespoons of water, then stir in sugar and ketchup.
  3. Core tomatoes and cut into 1/2 inch wide wedges.
  4. Heat skillet with vegetable oil, add scallions, cook until aromatic. Add eggs and cook further, set aside while still runny. Pour eggs into mixing bowl and wipe out pan.
  5. Cook ginger in reheated pan. Add tomatoes and salt to taste, cooking until flesh has softened.
  6. Reduce head, add cornstarch mixture into pan. Cook until it comes to a boil and thickens, stir in eggs for a few more seconds. Top with remaining scallions and serve with rice.
